AI & Research

AI-Powered Extension for Agricultural Resilience

Central Region 2026-2027

Harnessing AI to improve extension worker services for smallholder farmers. Developing an AI-powered Decision Support System using RAG technology to provide context-specific insights from Lesotho's ReNOKA Compendium for soil and water conservation.

Innovation: AI decision support • RAG technology • Sesotho translation • RLHF feedback loop

Research Partner: University of Virginia | Collaboration with National University of Lesotho
Water Development

LLWDP II Community Mobilization Study

Zones 2, 3, 6, 7 Oct 2025 – Feb 2026

Community empowerment and capacity building to ensure ownership, accountability, management and sustainability of water systems. Introducing Water Service Provision models as stipulated in the Water and Sanitation Services Bill and Customer Connection Strategy.

Services: Community mobilization • Capacity building • Baseline surveys • Stakeholder engagement

Client: Ministry of Natural Resources | JV with Khanyisa Projects
Agriculture

SADP II Southern Region Consultancy

Southern Region May 2025 – Jun 2026

National Service Provider supporting SADP II Matching Grants beneficiaries with technical assistance, grant implementation oversight, and administrative management of field service providers in the Southern Region.

Services: Technical assistance • Grant implementation • Service provider management • Reporting

Client: Ministry of Agriculture Food Security & Nutrition | Funded by World Bank (IDA 64220)
Agri-Tech

GrowSmart Solar-Powered Irrigation

Lesotho Ongoing

Revolutionary solar-powered, IoT-based irrigation system customized for each farm's agro-climatic conditions. Features high-efficiency solar panels, submersible pumps, customized drip irrigation, and mobile app integration for remote monitoring.

Features: Solar power • IoT sensors • Mobile app • Real-time data • Weather forecasts

In-house Innovation
Financial Inclusion

Gooi Gooi Savings Solutions

Lesotho Ongoing

Innovative savings system promoting financial inclusion for street vendors (KTB) and factory workers (MOB). Provides access to formal financial services, discounted groceries, financial literacy education, and cashback incentives.

Features: Digital savings • Discounted goods • Financial literacy • Cashback incentives

Partners: KTB, Mme-O-Bohlokoa, Business Apps
Climate Assessment

LHWP Climate Change Vulnerability Assessment

LHWP Catchment Completed

Comprehensive vulnerability assessment and adaptation strategy for Lesotho Highlands Water Project Phase I & II. Addressed climate impacts on infrastructure, communities, and environment within the catchment area.

Deliverables: Baseline study • Vulnerability assessment • Adaptation strategy • Mitigation strategy

Client: LHDA
Agriculture

Agricultural Insurance Feasibility Study

Lesotho Completed

Market study exploring smallholder agricultural insurance potential across 6 priority value chains: grains, dairy, horticulture, poultry, piggery, and fisheries/aquaculture. Included focus groups, stakeholder interviews, and data assessment.

Value Chains: Grains • Dairy • Horticulture • Poultry • Piggery • Fisheries

Partners: RMSI India | Funded by IFC/World Bank
Women Empowerment

Empowering Women Through Savings Groups

5 Districts Completed

Explorative study examining how 95 saving groups and 45 MSMEs could transition to agro-entrepreneurship using the Lesotho Impact Investment Fund. Piloted mobile-based digital bookkeeping app for financial management.

Reach: 95 saving groups • 45 MSMEs • 5 districts

Partners: Caritas Lesotho, Care for Basotho | Funded by MCC
Water Economics

Value of Water in Lesotho Study

Lesotho-wide Upcoming

Estimating the economic value of water across different uses in Lesotho—agricultural, industrial, and consumptive. Analyzing how value changes during drought versus abundant periods to inform conservation investment decisions.

Sectors: Agriculture • Manufacturing • Hydropower • Fishing • Tourism

Research Partner: University of Virginia
