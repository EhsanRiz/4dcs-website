FAQ

Common Questions

Is Lipalo free to use?
▼

Yes! Lipalo is currently free to use. We believe in making data accessible to everyone working on development in Lesotho.

How accurate is the data?
▼

We only use official sources like the Bureau of Statistics, World Bank, and UN agencies. Every response includes citations so you can verify the source.

Can I use this for official reports?
▼

Lipalo is great for research and quick reference. For official reports, we recommend verifying with original sources using the citations provided.

Do you offer custom AI solutions?
▼

Yes! We build custom AI solutions for development organizations, governments, and NGOs. Contact us to discuss your needs.

What data categories are covered?
▼

Health, Demographics, Economy, Employment, Education, Agriculture, Food Security, Energy, Environment, Justice, and Gender statistics.
