About the Project

The Lesotho Lowlands Water Development & Sanitation Project Phase III (LLWDSP III) extends Lesotho's lowlands water supply infrastructure to improve access to potable water and sanitation services for communities across the lowlands. The digital tools on this hub support the Project Implementation Unit (PIU) in delivering the project transparently and accountably.
