Core Service Areas

We deliver integrated solutions across four key areas to address climate challenges.

AI & Smart Data Systems

We empower communities with AI-powered decision support systems and innovative data platforms that address climate challenges and foster sustainable market growth.

For: Government agencies, Multilaterals, NGOs, Private sector
  • AI-powered decision support tools
  • Mobile data collection apps
  • Real-time monitoring dashboards
  • GIS mapping & predictive analytics

Capacity Building & Training

Tailored education programs in livelihoods, agriculture, WASH, and social protection, fostering climate-resilient skills and sustainable practices.

For: Farmers, Community groups, Field workers, Extension officers
  • Training of Trainers (ToT) programs
  • Community sensitization campaigns
  • Educational materials (bilingual)
  • Skills assessment & certification

Climate-Smart Solutions

Transforming industries with climate-smart solutions, promoting resilience and mitigation through eco-friendly practices and optimized supply chains.

For: Agribusinesses, Cooperatives, Private sector, Farmer groups
  • Vulnerability assessments
  • Adaptation & mitigation strategies
  • Solar irrigation systems
  • IoT-based monitoring solutions

Strategic Partnerships

Maximizing local climate impact through strategic partnerships with leading development organizations and research institutions.

For: Multilaterals, Research institutions, Donors, Government
  • Joint venture project delivery
  • Research collaboration
  • Technical advisory services
  • Consortium management

Specialized Services

Digital Solutions
